Elijah Losch

Elijah Losch is a freelance extreme metal drummer based out of Portland, Oregon, who plays for numerous acts across the Pacific Northwest. Elijah began playing semi professionally at the age of 17 for symphonic black metal band Mortal Plague (now inactive). Since then Elijah has gained popularity amongst the metal community playing for other acts such as Silver Talon, The Devils of Loudun, Divitius, and many more. Elijah has multiple endorsements including Vratim, Czarcie Kopyto, and Scorpion.
